A SAMPLING OF THE DOZENS OF “SUCCESS STORIES” AMONG JUSTICE BRIDGE’S REFERRALS… IN THE ATTORNEYS’ OWN WORDS

CONSUMER PROTECTION

I regularly advise clients on used-automobile "lemon laws" which fall under the Consumer Protection Act. The best success stories are those cases which are able to settle after an initial demand letter is sent to avoid the necessity of trial. Here, the clients are generally without a working vehicle or are scared to drive a defective vehicle so a quick turnaround is important. I have sent successful demand letters to both small "shady" dealerships as well as the large corporate dealers with success. I have had dealers make the necessary repairs, accept the vehicle as a trade-in towards a new vehicle at a reduced sale price, and refund the complete purchase price.

EMPLOYMENT LAW

Client “SK” filed a charge of discrimination based on race with the Massachusetts Commission Against Discrimination. We attended an investigative conference and submitted a response to the employer’s position statement. We brought the matter to MCAD-facilitated mediation after determining that the employer was giving negative references to potential employers for over a year and a half, and SK received $15,000 in settlement,as well as a written agreement that the employer would only verify the dates of SK’s employment to prospective employers. After this settlement agreement, SK was able to obtain full-time employment within two months.

Dispute Resolution & Legal Advisory

A small business owner found herself entangled in a complicated legal dispute that threatened her financial stability. Understanding the potential for significant losses, I reviewed the case and provided her with clear legal guidance. By leading a series of focused discussions with the opposing party, we achieved an amicable resolution that protected her assets and saved her from prolonged litigation. The successful outcome gave her peace of mind, and she expressed her deep gratitude. It’s moments like these that reinforce my dedication to helping clients resolve disputes efficiently and equitably.

FAMILY LAW

A Medfield client had litigated a divorce modification pro se and lost the case. The judgment contained scathing commentary about him and a suspended jail sentence if he failed to pay on time and in full as the court had ordered. The client had a payment due that he could not afford and sought legal assistance from Justice Bridge to determine his options. After a detailed review of the documents and significant consultation with the client, the client sought financial help from various sources to secure some sort of payment. I then negotiated different payment terms with the opposing party's attorney and succeeded in part because a payment was available. A stipulation was then filed with the court.

Contract Negotiation and Risk Mitigation

A local entrepreneur faced a critical contract issue that could have severely impacted her business. The stakes were high, and the risk of financial loss loomed large. I stepped in, carefully analyzing the situation and collaborating with both parties to devise a solution. Through skillful negotiations, we were able to revise the contract terms, eliminating any risk while enhancing her financial standing. The business owner was incredibly relieved, knowing her business could move forward without the fear of legal repercussions. As a gesture of appreciation, she expressed heartfelt thanks, a reminder of the value of providing strategic guidance in times of need.

Business Continuity Strategy

When a small business owner was facing a potential breach of contract that could have interrupted her operations, I stepped in to provide a strategic solution. After conducting a thorough review of her contractual obligations, I worked with both parties to craft a solution that would avoid any costly delays. We renegotiated terms that allowed her to keep the business running while addressing all concerns. The owner was relieved to continue operations smoothly and thanked me for my swift intervention. Helping clients maintain business continuity in the face of challenges is something I truly value.
