WHO WE ARE

ACE & ERNEST is a community of lawyers dedicated to providing legal services to persons and businesses that do not qualify for free legal services but cannot afford standard market rates. All of the attorneys in our network are members in good standing of the Massachusetts bar. Justice Bridge is not a law firm. It is a network of lawyers striving to provide meaningful legal help within their clients’ ability to pay. We have offices in downtown Boston and New Bedford.

OUR VISION

We believe the U.S. legal system was established to protect everyone's rights
That is why the lawyers who work with the ACE & ERNEST Legal Center provide
professional legal advice at reduced rates for people with modest incomes.
We offer a range of legal expertise. Lawyers work with their clients to provide the best
possible services within their budgets.

RESTORING HOPE

OUR Team emphasizes that Justice Bridge Legal Center is about restoring hope—for attorneys desiring autonomy in their law practices and for individuals fearing that legal advice is beyond their means. We operate on the zeal of our attorneys, leverage the latest in technology, draw from the experience of over 80 mentors averaging over 30 years of legal experience, and foster a collaborative spirit within our community.

BRIDGING THE GAP

Most of our Justice Bridge attorneys are certified in limited-scope representation.
They may be able to represent you in limited stages in your legal proceeding – those
stages having the most potential impact on your legal matter. They might draft certain
legal documents, handle individual court appearances or negotiations, or just coach
you to represent yourself
